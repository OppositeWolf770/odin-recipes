Pizza

Lasagna is very good.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Shape the batter into a pizza crust.
  2. spread the marinara sauce over the crust.
  3. Put the cheese on over the sauce.
  4. Butter up the crust.
  5. Preheat the oven to 400deg.
  6. Put pizza in the oven for about 20 minutes.
  7. Take out, and enjoy!
